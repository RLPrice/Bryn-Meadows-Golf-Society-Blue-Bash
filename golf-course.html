"Enhance Your Golf Experience at Bryn Meadows"


Practice your swing and elevate your golf game at our championship golf facility, Bryn Meadows. Established in 1973, our parkland course provides a unique challenge at every tee, ensuring an engaging experience for all levels of players, from beginners to seasoned professionals.


Golf Amidst Natural Beauty


Nestled amidst rolling hills and tranquil streams, our golf courses enjoy a picturesque setting that allows you to focus solely on perfecting your game. Free from distractions, you can develop your strategic prowess and master your golf techniques while surrounded by the serene beauty of nature.


Championship Golf Course


Our par 71 championship golf course was meticulously designed by Brian Mayo and Craig Defoy. Its creation dates back to 1973, and since then, it has been a hub for golf enthusiasts. Featuring a distinctive challenge at every tee, no two holes are alike. Golfers have the opportunity to fine-tune their power, accuracy, and flexibility. Two standout holes, the 2nd with its 557-yard par five adjacent to out-of-bounds and the 13th with a 227-yard par three over water, are known for their intriguing challenges.


Academy Golf Course


Aspiring golfers, including juniors, are invited to refine their skills on our 9-hole academy golf course, established in 2014. This facility, which is the largest junior golf academy in Wales, is supported by professional golf coaches. Beginners can use this course to master the fundamentals before venturing onto the championship course or entering their first competition. Learning at your own pace has never been easier than on our academy golf course.


State-of-the-Art Golf Simulator


For a virtual golf experience like no other, our state-of-the-art golf simulator recreates courses from around the world. From the challenging links of Carnoustie and the prestigious fairways of Wentworth to the historic Royal Troon and the exotic landscapes of Abu Dhabi, we offer a wide selection of courses. Our simulator employs high-quality graphics to faithfully represent your shots, making it suitable for both aspiring golfers and seasoned professionals. Test your skills on some of the world's most iconic holes and enjoy an immersive golf adventure at Bryn Meadows.

Golf Breaks

Bryn Meadows stands as the ultimate choice for an extraordinary golf break in South Wales. Here, amidst the breathtaking beauty of the countryside, your holiday transforms into an unforgettable adventure. Picture yourself surrounded by the serene hills and lush landscapes that define Wales, creating the perfect backdrop for your golf getaway.

Golf Lessons

Our golf lessons cater to all levels of expertise and are open to members, golf enthusiasts from other clubs, and non-members alike. You'll receive personalized instruction from a fully qualified PGA professional, whether you're starting from scratch or focusing on specific areas for improvement. Lessons take place at various premium locations, including the Championship Golf Course, First Swing Golf Academy, Indoor Putting Studio, and the cutting-edge GC Quad Golf Simulator. Elevate your golf game with the expertise of our PGA Professional.

Golf Facilities

At Bryn Meadows, we're dedicated to providing top-notch golf facilities that cater to golfers of all levels. In addition to our exceptional golf courses, we offer an array of amenities to enhance your overall experience. Whether you're in the market for a new sports bag or require expert regripping services for your golf clubs, we've got you covered to ensure you're fully equipped for a stellar round of golf. And when it's time to unwind and celebrate your victories, our inviting bar and restaurant await. Raise a pint and savor delicious fare after conquering the greens. At Bryn Meadows, we're committed to delivering a comprehensive golfing experience, on and off the course.
